Bench Press

Specifications:

800 lbs. real weight capacity
2019 Upgraded 2" Wider Backrest/Seat
Strong powder coated tubular steel frame construction
Incline, flat and decline positions
Independent backrest adjustments
Can be folded for storage
Transportation wheels
3 Position Adjustable Leg Hold Down

How to do?

Lie on the bench with your eyes under the bar
Grab the bar with a medium grip-width (thumbs around the bar!)
Unrack the bar by straightening your arms
Lower the bar to your mid-chest
Press the bar back up until your arms are straight
